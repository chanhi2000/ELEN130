 ## 1.
__EXAMPLE: FAMILIARITY WITH $$W_N$$.__

Recall:
$$
W_N=e^{-j\tfrac{2\pi}{N}}
$$

### 1(a)
What is $${W_N}^{N}$$
$$
\begin{align*}
{W_{N}}^N&=e^{-j2\pi\tfrac{(N)}{N}}\\
&=e^{-j2\pi}\\&=1;
\end{align*}
$$

### 1(b)
What is $$W_N^{\tfrac{N}{2}}$$
$$
\begin{align*}
{W_{N}}^{\tfrac{N}{2}}&=e^{-j2\pi\tfrac{\left(\tfrac{N}{2}\right)}{N}}\\
&=e^{-j\pi}
\\&=-1;
\end{align*}
$$

### 1(c)
What is $${W_N}^{k+N}$$
$$
\begin{align*}
{W_{N}}^{k+N}&=e^{-j2\pi\tfrac{(N)}{N}}e^{-j2\pi\tfrac{(k)}{N}}\\
&=(1)e^{-j2\pi\tfrac{k}{N}}\\
&={W_N}^{k};
\end{align*}
$$
