## 3.
Assume you have a sequence $$\begin{matrix}x[n]=\{1,\:3,\:5,\:7,\:9\},&n=0:4\end{matrix}$$. What is the circularly shifted sequence: $$y[n]=x[\left<n-2\right>_5]$$?


| $$n$$ | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
| :---: | - | - | - | - | - |
| $$x[n]$$ | 1 | 3 | 5 | 7 | 9 |
| $$x[\left<n-2\right>_5]$$ | 7 | 9 | 1 | 3 | 5 |

$$
\begin{matrix}
\therefore\:y[n]=\{7,\:9,\:1,\:3,\:5\},&n=0:4
\end{matrix}
$$
